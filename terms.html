The driver must:

A refundable security deposit is required at the time of booking:

All vehicles are provided with a full fuel tank. You must return the vehicle with a full fuel tank. If returned on empty or with low fuel:

  • Empty tank: Full tank refill charge (₹2,500 - ₹4,500 depending on vehicle)
  • Partial tank: Charge as per fuel consumed at ₹140-160 per liter

5. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancellation up to 24 hours before booking: Full refund

  • No-show: 50% charge

For detailed refund information, visit our Refund Policy page.

Comprehensive insurance is included in all rentals covering third-party liability. However, the renter is liable for:

  • Minor damages: Deducted from security deposit

Documentation of damage condition is mandatory at pickup and drop-off.
