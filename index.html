Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our car rental service

What documents do I need to book a car with RoyalGoaRide?

You'll need a valid driver's license (international license recommended for foreign travelers), passport/ID proof, and a credit card for security deposit. For Indian residents, Aadhar, Driving License, and Passport are acceptable. The deposit is refundable after safe return of the vehicle.

Do you offer automatic transmission cars?

Yes! We offer both automatic and manual transmission options. Our fleet includes automatic variants of Maruti Swift, Ignis, Baleno, Hyundai i20, and Mahindra Thar. Automatic cars are slightly higher in price but perfect for those who prefer ease of driving in Goa's traffic.

What is the security deposit required?

The security deposit is ₹3,000 for cars and ₹1,500 for bikes. This is held as a guarantee against any damages or violations. The full amount is refunded within 48 hours of vehicle return if there are no issues. Minor wear and tear is covered in the deposit.

Do you offer airport pickup and delivery?

Absolutely! We offer airport pickup and delivery at Dabolim Airport (North Goa) and Madgaon Railway Station. Our staff will meet you with your rental vehicle, explain the features, and arrange the handover. We accommodate flights across all times - early morning or late night.

How long does it take to get a car after booking?

We ensure delivery within 30-45 minutes if you're in Margao, Madgaon, or nearby areas. For airport pickup, we'll arrange delivery based on your flight arrival time. Same-day bookings are possible if made before 10 AM. For instant queries, use our WhatsApp booking option.
