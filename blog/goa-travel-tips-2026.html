Goa travel tips 2026

Visit Goa during November to February for pleasant weather. Rent a self-drive car to explore beyond typical tourist spots—discover Morjim for migratory birds, Old Goa for heritage, and Patnem for quiet beaches. Early morning beach drives offer solitude; evening visits capture stunning sunsets. Carry sunscreen, stay hydrated, and use local roads for authentic experiences.
