Self drive vs taxi Goa

Choosing between self drive and taxi in Goa depends on your needs. Self-driving offers flexibility to explore at your pace, visit hidden beaches, and save money on long trips. Taxis provide comfort if you prefer not to drive. For exploring Goa's diverse attractions—from Dudhsagar Falls to remote beaches—self drive car rental is more economical and gives you freedom.
