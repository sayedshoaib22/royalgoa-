Best cars for Goa trip

Planning a Goa trip? Choose vehicles that balance comfort, fuel efficiency and size. For families, 7-seaters like Ertiga or Innova provide space. For couples and solo travellers, hatchbacks like Swift or Baleno offer great mileage and easy parking. For beach drives and adventure, SUVs with higher ground clearance are ideal.
