🔒 Security Deposit Explained

Security deposit is temporary money held to protect the vehicle. Here's how it works:

  • Amount: ₹2,000–₹5,000 depending on vehicle
  • How Paid: Credit card authorization (no charges)
  • When Refunded: Within 24 hours after car return
  • Deductions: Only if vehicle has damage beyond normal wear & tear
  • Documentation: Vehicle inspection report given at pickup & return

The deposit protects both you and RoyalGoaRide. No surprise charges!
